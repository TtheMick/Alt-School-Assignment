Ononye Michael Chukwuebuka

Ononye Michael Chukwuebuka is a 25-year-old recent graduate of Medical Laboratory Science from Imo State University, Nigeria, and is currently a medical intern. Hailing from Anambra State, Nigeria, Michael comes from a large family of eight, where he is the second eldest among six siblings. His academic journey has been marked by a dedication to the sciences, particularly in the medical field. However, Michael's strong skills in computer and technology literacy have inspired him to expand his horizons beyond healthcare. This passion for technology led him to join Alt School, where he is pursuing further education in the School of Engineering with a focus on becoming a cloud engineer.

Michael's goals in the School of Engineering program include mastering cloud computing technologies, contributing to the development of scalable and efficient cloud solutions, and applying his technical expertise to innovate in the field. He is driven by a desire to integrate his technological skills with engineering principles to solve complex challenges and make a significant impact in the tech industry.

Outside of his professional and academic pursuits, Michael enjoys playing football, a hobby that helps him stay active and maintain a balanced lifestyle. As he continues to advance in his multifaceted career, Michael remains committed to excellence and eager to make a meaningful contribution to the world of cloud engineering.
